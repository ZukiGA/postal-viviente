# Travel Story Template ✈️

Document trips with 7 narrative prompts.

---

## Frontmatter Schema

```yaml
---
title: "Trip Name / Destination"
slug: "trip-destination"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
published: false
template: "travel-story"
tags:
  - travel
  - destination
description: "Trip summary"
location:
  city: "City"
  country: "Country"
gallery:
  - photo1.jpg
---
```

---

## Screen 1 - Title & Details

**Prompt:**  
Destination, dates, travel companions (or solo).

---

## Screen 2 - The Journey

**Prompt:**  
Getting there. How was the trip itself?

---

## Screen 3 - Arrival

**Prompt:**  
First impression when you arrived.

---

## Screen 4 - Highlights

**Prompt:**  
Best moments/experiences (3-5 bullet points).

---

## Screen 5 - People

**Prompt:**  
People you met or interactions that stood out.

---

## Screen 6 - Unexpected

**Prompt:**  
What surprised you? What didn't go as planned (good or bad)?

---

## Screen 7 - Takeaway

**Prompt:**  
What will you remember most? What did you learn?
