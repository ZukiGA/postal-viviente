# City Exploration Template 🌆

Document urban walks and city discoveries with 6 prompts.

---

## Frontmatter Schema

```yaml
---
title: "Walking [Neighborhood/Area]"
slug: "walking-neighborhood"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
published: false
template: "city-exploration"
tags:
  - walk
  - neighborhood
  - urban-exploration

  neighborhood: "Neighborhood"
  city: "City"
gallery:
  - photo1.jpg
---
```

---

## Screen 1 - Start

**Prompt:**  
Where did you start? What time? What was the plan (or lack of)?

---

## Screen 2 - Discoveries

**Prompt:**  
What did you find? Unexpected moments along the way.

---

## Screen 3 - Best Moment

**Prompt:**  
Highlight of the walk. What was your favorite photo/moment? Why this one?

---

## Screen 4 - Reflections

**Prompt:**  
What were you thinking? Thoughts while walking.

---

## Screen 5 - New Perspective

**Prompt:**  
What did you learn? New perspective gained.

---
